Q&A with North Forty


What drone do you have? I have a DJI Phantom 4
What is drone videography / photography? Drone Aerial photography is flying a unmanned aerial vehicle in the air and recording and taking pictures from above. It adds a different view then what we are use to.
What kinds of events do you do? I do all sorts of events. I’ve filmed wake surfing, mountain biking, ATV’s, weddings. If someone has an idea then I can make that idea a reality.
How does your pricing work? I currently have several packages for different projects. Contact me for pricing.
What sets you apart from the competition? I would say my knowledge and skills of photography/videography. I’ve studied several years at UVU about this topic plus hours and hours of real life experience. I am able to turn any idea/dream into a reality.
